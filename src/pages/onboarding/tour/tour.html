Dallar is a type of,
cryptocurrency.

It's goal is to be a gesture of
appreciation in a thankless world.

Just scan someone's code to give!

Dallar is for game developers.

You can use it to buy game development resources or to support tutorial makers.

It's exact value fluctuates with the community and market.

You control your Dallar.

This app stores your Dallar with cutting-edge security.
